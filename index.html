Yuxiang Hong

洪宇翔

Hangzhou · China· hongyx@hdu.edu.cn

Yuxiang Hong is an Associate Professor in the School of Management at Hangzhou Dianzi University. He has earned Ph.D. degree in Management Science and Engineering from Jinan University, Guangzhou, China (2014). His previous experience includes working as a visiting scholar in Utrecht School of Governance at Utrecht University in the Netherland (2012-2013), and Centre for Security, Communications and Network Research at Plymouth University in the UK (2018-2019).

risk analysis, emergency management, digital governance, technology policy
