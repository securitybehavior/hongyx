Yuxiang Hong

洪宇翔

Hangzhou · China· hongyx@hdu.edu.cn

Yuxiang Hong is an Associate Professor at Hangzhou Dianzi University (HDU), Hangzhou, P.R.China. He obtained his Ph.D in Management Science and Engineering at Jinan University, Guangzhou, P.R.China. He was a joint training PhD candidate at Utrecht University (2012-2013), and an academic visitor at University of Plymouth (2018-2019). He also short-term visitied University of Oxford and Jeju National University.。

Study area
Yuxiang Hong's research focuses on the interdisciplinary studies combining environment, human capital, emergencing technology, and risk. Research areas include: human aspect of information security, emergency preparedness, risk communication, environmental psychology, green innovation.
